Interests & Exploratory Projects

Representation in Computer Vision

Outside of formal projects, I’m interested in how bias and representation appear in computer vision systems. This interest comes partly from cosplay, where I’ve noticed a disconnect between the diversity of real people and the way subjects are represented in many commonly used image datasets for machine learning.

I’m exploring the idea of building an open, responsibly sourced image dataset focused on Black cosplayers. The project would examine how dataset composition and labeling choices affect model performance and bias, while also being intentional about consent, documentation, and ethical data use.

  • Computer Vision
  • Dataset Design
  • Ethical AI
  • Bias & Representation
